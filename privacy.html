Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [Date]

1. Information We Collect

HealthScore AI collects the following types of information:

  • Food Images: Photos you take or select for analysis are processed by our AI service to provide nutritional information. Images are processed securely and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Location Data: If you choose to enable location services, we collect location information to tag your meals. This data is stored locally on your device and synced to iCloud if you have iCloud enabled.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, including meal analysis frequency and feature usage.
  • Profile Information: Optional profile information such as height, weight, and BMI calculations are stored locally on your device and synced to iCloud.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered food analysis services
  • To track and display your meal history and statistics
  • To improve our AI algorithms and service quality
  • To improve user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using Core Data. If you have iCloud enabled, your data is synced securely to your personal iCloud account. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information during transmission and storage.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: Food images are processed by Google's Gemini AI service to generate nutritional analysis. Images are sent securely and processed according to Google's privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat: For in-app purchase and subscription management. RevenueCat processes payment information according to their privacy policy.
  • Apple iCloud: For data synchronization if you enable iCloud. Data is stored in your personal iCloud account and subject to Apple's privacy policy.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Delete your data at any time by deleting the app or clearing app data
  • Opt out of location tracking in your device settings
  • Manage your privacy settings in the app

6. Children's Privacy

HealthScore AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
